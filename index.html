Our foundation

Independent journalism for an informed, empowered society. A world where truth is accessible to everyone, power is held accountable, and every voice has a platform.

Mission

To produce rigorous, fact‑based reporting on politics, technology, culture, and investigations — with transparency, independence, and deep accountability to the public, not to advertisers or political interests.

About PADECO NEWS

PADECO NEWS is an independent journalism platform founded and run by Bush Smith — a reporter dedicated to uncovering the truth through rigorous, investigative, and solutions‑oriented reporting.

Bush covers politics, technology, culture, and investigations with a focus on accountability, transparency, and the public interest. Every story is fact‑checked, sourced, and independent. No sponsors, no political ties.
